About L’orto pasta

At L’orto Pasta, we are passionate about good Italian
cuisine made with high quality ingredients. Five years ago,
we were inspired to create a restaurant with the atmosphere
of an Italian trattoria with dishes that are both traditional
and innovative. L’orto Pasta is a place to gather and make
memories over delicious food.
